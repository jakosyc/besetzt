Hello

This interactive map of vienna shows the History of House Occupation in Vienna.

House Occupations are political! Activists often use them as a tool to raise awareness for social issues & amplify the voices of the unheard.

Please be aware that this is sensible Information for some & use it with care!

Thank you.

What is this?

This is an interactive map, showing the History of House Occupation in Vienna.
You can click on the blue markers to learn more about each location and the Story of the Building.
All Sources of the background research and the images are listed in the popup of each marker.

Please be aware that this is an ongoing research Project and that the list will always be incomplete.
House Occupations have a long & not always well documented History in Vienna. There are many Buildings missing in this Map.

I Apologize if mistakes in the Research are Made and ask for corrections anytime!
Also if you have more Information or Questions you can contact me via the small + in the top right corner of the Website.

This Map is a project in the Framework of a Architecture mapping seminar at the University of fine Arts Vienna.
It was html coded & the code is Open Source. Its acessible via Github.

*2025. Jakob Noah Sycha.
